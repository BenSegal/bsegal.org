NLS Spectrum Applet

Run using Mathematica CDF Player. Free download available here: http://www.wolfram.com/cdf-player/

Compatible with:

  • Windows: Internet Explorer & Firefox
  • Mac: Safari & Firefox

    • If the web applet does not work, you can also download the CDF here: NLSSpectrum.cdf and play on your own machine.

    Drag the slider through in parameter space to view the linear stability spectrum for solutions with those parameters.
